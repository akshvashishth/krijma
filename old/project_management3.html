Project Management / Facility Management

In the scope of facility management, We at Krijma focus on providing CUSTOMISED SOLUTIONS. We provide following services to our esteemed clients:

A Operation & Maintenance:
•
To take care of routine operation and maintenance of building , Engineering staff like Supervisors/ Technicians are deployed for the services like DG set Operation, Air conditioning Operation and Plumbing & Fire-fighting Operations.
•
They take care of routine/ preventive/ break down maintenance of equipments.
B General Administration & Housekeeping:
•
To maintain a healthy relation in between the lessor and lessee of the building, we deploy the facility manager to make day to day interactions on all the issues related to facilities. He also takes care of up keeping of building premises as neat & clean with the help of house keeping team.
C Energy/ Maintenance Billing:
•
Keeping in mind the variations in input cost like variation in diesel prices/ labour cost, we evaluate the variation in maintenance charges and accordingly owner is advised for new charges.
D Optimization:
  To maintain the principle of Economy , Excellence and Ethics, we do the auditing of Equipments/ Building Systems for its efficient utilization. It consists of audit of energy consumption, scheduling of equipments & men power for effective use.
