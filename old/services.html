For industrial, residential and commercial customers, we are able to design & execution of your mains distribution, power supply or lighting installations, together with statutory requirements such as emergency lighting and fire alarm systems. We can also advise on energy conservation, "getting the best" out of your electrical services. We work alongside your builders and other contractors, and ensure any work is carried out with the minimum of disruption.

•
We are specialize in providing & execution of Electrical Installations. viz. H.T. Works, L.T. Works, Earthing and Lightning Protection etc.
•
HT Works: We provide supply, installation, testing & commissioning for 11 KV power including laying of under ground & over head lines.
•
LT Works: We provide supply , installation, testing & commissioning for LT work i.e laying of cables, installation of Panel Boards , supply & fixing of lighting fixtures, completion of Conduiting, wiring & lighting fixtures in any kind of interior job.
•
Telephone & LAN: We provide complete system pertaining to the Telephone and LAN viz. Server Racks, Data Switches, CAT-6 cabling, IOs, Face Plates and Telephone Instruments etc.
•
Fire Alarm System : We provide complete system pertaining to the Fire Alarm System viz. Addressable and Conventional Panels, Smoke Detectors, Heat Detectors, Response Indicators, Hooters, MCPs, Cabling and Repeater Panels etc.
•
CCTV & Access Control System: We provide entire solution for monitoring the activities of people in the crucial locations to avoid any mishap. And also we provide the solution pertaining to the Access of People with their Individual I.D. Card by Integrating properly with the systems.
