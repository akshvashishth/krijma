KRIJMA Engineers Pvt Ltd. is the optimum amalgamation of People, experience, and professionalism to give life to your imagination. It is a worthy name in the field of engineering to provide wide solutions under one roof related to Civil, Electrical and Mechanical services. We at KRIJMA work as professionals / contractors and service provider offering project management, facility management and execution of Civil, Electrical, Mechanical Projects. The company can accommodate a single-phase project or deliver a complete turnkey job.

Established in January, 2009, with a sole objective of providing quality engineering solutions to Real Estate Sector with a rich experience and specialization in Engineering & Construction, Electrical & HVAC, Project & Facility Management services. We partner with companies to formulate, implement and execute their global electrical, mechanical and civil engineering strategies. We believe that self-reliance with a flexible approach play key role in success of organization. Every aspect of Company's functioning points towards total customer satisfaction. We also believe in staying close to the clients & assisting them in completely utilizing their resources, increasing productivity, optimizing the resources sharing, identifying & planning the information technology needs. Our experience includes domestic and international sites.
